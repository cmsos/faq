Notations and abbreviations

To minimize use of special symbols we denote Ao=Å, deg=degree (angle), iX=X^(-1), X/mol=X per molecule, dX=deviation of X.

  • EA Electron Affinity
  • IE Ionization Energy (Ionization Potential, IP)
  • DFT Density Functional Theory
  • DFT-D Dispersion-corrected DFT
  • HOMO Highest Occupied MO
  • LUMO Lowest Unoccupied MO
  • MO Molecular Orbital
  • LMO Localized MO
